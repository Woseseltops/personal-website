Science

  • Stoop & Van den Bosch (2014). Using ideolects to asdfsdf.
    A short paper that describes how you can improve text prediction for a person by training the predictor on his/her own written texts (an idea we call ´idiolect´, taken from sociolinguistics). As an example, we predict tweets of a person by training on the earlier tweets of that same person. Interestingly, friends talk that much alike that you can also train on the tweets of friends and have even better predictions.
